## Challenge Reveal

Six challenge briefs · Three tracks · One goal: build AI that matters.

### High School Track — AI for Everyday Good

**Brief 1 — Community**: "Help is Hard to Find" — Make Support Obvious
Design AI tools that help people find support, understand information, and feel confident asking for help.
- Direction A: Crisis-to-Action Translator
- Direction B: Rumor vs. Reality Tracker

**Brief 2 — Environment**: "Make Climate Action Local & Real" — Tools People Use
Make environmental impact visible and local action practical, measurable, and engaging.
- Direction A: Food Waste Rescue Radar
- Direction B: My School's Hidden Footprint

### Undergraduate Track — AI for Life & Work

**Brief 3 — Productivity**: Build the "Second Brain" for Real Life
Help people make better decisions, manage overload, and turn complexity into clear action.
- Direction A: Life Decision Simulator
- Direction B: Zero-to-One Builder

**Brief 4 — Public Services**: Fix Systems People Depend On
Make essential systems — housing, benefits, healthcare — easier to understand and navigate.
- Direction A: Benefits Navigator
- Direction B: Housing Stability Guide

### Graduate Track — AI for Systems & Society

**Brief 5 — Human Safety & Protection**: Build AI Systems That Protect People
Detect risks earlier, strengthen protection networks, and support safer intervention strategies for vulnerable populations.
- Direction A: AI Against Digital Exploitation
- Direction B: Economic Recovery Pathways for Survivors

**Brief 6 — Public Systems & Policy**: Help Communities Make Better Decisions
Model outcomes, simulate policy interventions, and help institutions make evidence-based decisions.
- Direction A: The Cost of Doing Nothing Simulator
- Direction B: Community AI Readiness Blueprint
